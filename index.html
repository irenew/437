Project Overview:

By definition, when there is excessive amount of noise that disrupts the natural balance would be considered as noise pollution. Noise pollution within a household is one of the most underrated form of pollution, and it is often caused by excessive use of electronic households and water. It not only affect the environment but also humanâ€™s health state (hearing impairment, sleep disturbances). Moreover, People tend to react to noise pollution in form of producing more noise, rather than attempting to lower the noise level. This is why we are producing a kinetic sculpture that mimics how the nature would communicate to us about noise pollution within the indoor environment. To promote sustainable living, our goal of this project is to promote awareness regarding the usage of water and electricity within the household.
